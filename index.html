Why I Want to Be a Front-End Developer and How HNG Will Help Me Achieve My Goals

My Motivation for Becoming a Front-End Developer

The web has always fascinated me. The ability to create interactive and aesthetically pleasing websites, web apps, and mobile apps that people can access from anywhere in the world excites me. My journey into front-end development started with a simple question: how do websites work? This curiosity led me to download "Mimo," a mobile app where I learned the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My learning journey took a significant turn when I connected with a mentor from India through Twitter. They guided me to valuable resources where I could practice my skills, leading me to build my first simple websites. The experience of creating something from scratch with just HTML and CSS was incredibly rewarding. The ability to transform a blank screen into a functional, beautiful interface gave me a sense of accomplishment I had never experienced before.

My Learning Journey

Though I faced challenges balancing JavaScript learning with my studies, I persevered and eventually progressed to React, which revolutionized how I approached web development. The component-based architecture and state management concepts opened up new possibilities in how I could structure and build applications. Currently, I'm expanding my skillset with Tailwind CSS and Next.js, constantly pushing my boundaries in the frontend space.
